About Me

I'm a Mechanical Engineer with hands-on experience in product design, simulation, and innovation—currently working in the Research & Innovation (Compressor) department at Walton Hi-Tech Industries PLC. I specialize in advanced CAD modeling, GD&T-based drafting, structural and CFD analysis, and dynamic simulation.

With a strong foundation in SolidWorks, CATIA, Siemens NX, STAR-CCM+ and ANSYS, I bring ideas to life through design validation, additive manufacturing, and performance benchmarking. I actively contribute to cross-functional collaboration and support problem-solving initiatives in fast-paced R&D environments.

Skills & Core Components:

  • 3D modeling and 2D drafting with precise implementation of GD&T standards using SOLIDWORKS
  • Structural and modal analysis using Ansys Workbench
  • CFD analysis for gas flow/force using Ansys Fluent
  • Lubrication Flow Simulations (CFD Analysis) using "Simcenter STAR-CCM+"
  • Hands-on experience in rapid prototyping and design validation through 3D printing

Interests: CAD and Digital Manufacturing | Mechanical System Optimization | Machine Design | Additive Manufacturing | CFD
